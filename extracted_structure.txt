P: Our school follows an English medium curriculum, ensuring students are equipped with strong language skills, scientific temper, and a love for lifelong learning.
P: 35+ qualified teachers with deep subject expertise and passion for nurturing young minds across all grade levels.
P: Complete English medium instruction from Nursery to 10th, preparing students for competitive exams and higher education.
P: Smart classrooms, science labs, computer labs, library, and sports grounds for all-round development.
P: Every stage of your child's academic journey, expertly guided from the very beginning.
P: We are more than just a school — we are a community committed to the holistic growth of every child.
P: State-of-the-art infrastructure designed to support every aspect of a student's educational journey.
P: Structured learning pathways tailored for each stage — from early childhood to secondary board preparation.
P: We welcome students of all backgrounds. Our admission process is simple, transparent, and guided by a student-first philosophy.
P: Call us or visit the school between 9 AM – 5 PM (Mon–Sat) to express interest and learn about available seats for your child's class.
P: Tour our campus, meet the teachers, and let your child interact with our faculty. This helps both families and school assess mutual suitability.
P: Provide Birth Certificate, previous year's Marks Card/TC, Aadhar card, and 4 passport photos for admission registration.
P: Complete fee payment and receive your Welcome Kit. Your child is officially a Sri Chaithanya student — the journey begins!
